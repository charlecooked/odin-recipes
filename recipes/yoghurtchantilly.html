Yoghurt Chantilly

To Die For Yoghurt Chantilly

You can use this for desserts. Put it on everything!

Prep time: 5 minutes Total time: 10 minutes Yield: 20 small portions (soup spoons)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups creme liquid
  • ½ cup powdered sugar
  • 2 cups yaourt Grec

Steps

  1. Put the cream and the powdered sugar into the mixer and whip until it holds nice peaks.
  2. Add in the yaourt Grec and whip until combined.
  3. Pour into a short ⅓ bac and keep in the fridge. Don’t forget to date it.
  4. The yaourt chantilly will last quite a few days if it isn’t brought in and out of the fridge.
